Our Mission

Chemicals Free

Our treatments are Formaldehyde Toluene, and Dibutyl Phthalate (DBP) free, with Formaldehyde Resin and Camphor free options available.

Environment Friendly

As a participant of the Healthy Nail Salon program, our focus is on environmentally friendly products.Our packaging is 100% reusable, and our products are locally sourced whenever available.

Easy Schedulling

With the help of our friends at Vagaro (link here), scheduling has never been easy. Vagaro allows our clients to schedule on the go.

Organics with Love

Our Organic and Natural scrubs are made in house with organic brown sugar, organic oils, 100% Natural Shea Butter, Pink Himalayan salt blended with dry ginger and essential oils.
